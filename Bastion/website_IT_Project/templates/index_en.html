Avantguard
Features of implementation The technical platform automatically displays analytical data on mobile and stationary devices in the On-line mode, followed by the possibility of responding in automatic or manual modes. The platform is deployed on one or more servers, which can be both physical and virtual machines. The technological platform runs under the Astra Linux operating system. Deployment and usage of the platform components with other Linux, FreeBSD and Windows distributions is possible. The system architecture allows you to place individual services on different servers, which provides scalability and controlled distribution. There is a mechanism of service redundancy, in case of unavailability of some resources the system automatically rebuilds information flows. The practical effect of implementation
  1. Ability to refuse panel protection services and pay only for the response.
  2. The implementation of the platform allows you to get reliable information about what is happening at the site in real time, thereby eliminating dependence on external contractors and own employees.
  3. The ability to remotely access, respond to, manage and analyze information from anywhere in the world where there is internet.
  4. All equipment and access to the platform can be hosted on the Customer's premises or can be provided as a cloud service.
  5. The equipment can be easily moved and connected at other sites.
  6. Monitoring the status of equipment and systems connected to the technology platform.
  7. Minimization of labor costs for maintenance and process support
